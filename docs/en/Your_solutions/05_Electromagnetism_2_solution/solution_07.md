# 🌈 Physics Basics: Wavelength and Frequency

## 1. Problem Statement
The human eye is most sensitive to light with a wavelength of about **$550 \, \text{nm}$**. 
1. What color does this correspond to in the visible spectrum?
2. What is the frequency ($f$) of this light?

---

## 2. Fundamental Physics Principles

### A. The Visible Spectrum
The visible spectrum ranges from approximately $400 \, \text{nm}$ (Violet) to $700 \, \text{nm}$ (Red). Different wavelengths are perceived by the human brain as different colors.


### B. The Wave Equation
For all electromagnetic waves (including light), the speed, wavelength, and frequency are related by the formula:
$$c = f \cdot \lambda$$

Where:
* **$c$**: Speed of light in vacuum ($\approx 3.0 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}$)
* **$f$**: Frequency (measured in Hertz, $\text{Hz}$)
* **$\lambda$ (Lambda)**: Wavelength (measured in meters, $\text{m}$)

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Identify the Color
Light with a wavelength of **$550 \, \text{nm}$** falls right in the middle of the visible spectrum.
* **Color:** This wavelength corresponds to **Green** (specifically a yellowish-green). 
* **Fun Fact:** Evolutionarily, our eyes became most sensitive to this color because it is the peak wavelength emitted by the Sun that reaches Earth's surface.

---

### Step 2: Convert Units
To use the wave equation, we must convert nanometers ($\text{nm}$) to meters ($\text{m}$):
$$550 \, \text{nm} = 550 \times 10^{-9} \, \text{m}$$
$$550 \, \text{nm} = 5.5 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{m}$$

---

### Step 3: Calculate the Frequency ($f$)
Rearrange the formula $c = f \cdot \lambda$ to solve for $f$:
$$f = \frac{c}{\lambda}$$

Substitute the values:
$$f = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8}{5.5 \times 10^{-7}}$$

**Calculation:**
$$3.0 / 5.5 \approx 0.5454$$
$$10^8 / 10^{-7} = 10^{15}$$
$$f \approx 0.545 \times 10^{15} \, \text{Hz}$$

Standard scientific notation:
**$f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14} \, \text{Hz}$** (or $545 \, \text{THz}$)

---

## 4. Final Results Summary
| Property | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Wavelength ($\lambda$)** | $550 \, \text{nm}$ |
| **Color** | **Green** |
| **Frequency ($f$)** | $5.45 \times 10^{14} \, \text{Hz}$ |
