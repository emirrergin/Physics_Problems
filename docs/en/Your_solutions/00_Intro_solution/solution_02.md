## 2. Systems of Equations — Solution

Given equations:

$$2x + 3y = 12 \quad (1)$$

$$x - y = 1 \quad (2)$$

---

### Step 1: Express $x$ in Terms of $y$ (from Equation 2)

$$x = 1 + y \quad (3)$$

---

### Step 2: Substitute (3) into Equation (1)

$$2(1 + y) + 3y = 12$$

$$2 + 2y + 3y = 12$$

$$5y = 10$$

$$\boxed{y = 2}$$

---

### Step 3: Solve for $x$

Substitute $y = 2$ back into (3):

$$x = 1 + 2 = \boxed{3}$$

---

### Verification

Check both equations with $x = 3,\ y = 2$:

$$2(3) + 3(2) = 6 + 6 = 12 \checkmark$$

$$3 - 2 = 1 \checkmark$$

---

### Result

$$\boxed{x = 3, \quad y = 2}$$
